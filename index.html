Welcome to Bench Warmers!

At Bench Warmers Sports Bar & Grill, we bring the game-day experience to you every day of the week. Whether you're here to catch the big game, enjoy great food, or hang out with friends, we’ve got the perfect spot waiting for you. With multiple big-screen TVs, ice-cold drinks, and a lively atmosphere, there’s no better place to kick back and relax.

Great Food. Cold Drinks. Good Times.

Our menu is packed with fan favorites—from crispy chicken strips and loaded fries to burgers, wings, and more. Pair your meal with one of our rotating draft beers or signature cocktails. Whether you're stopping in for a quick bite or staying all night, we’ve got something for everyone.

Events & Entertainment

There’s always something happening at Bench Warmers. Join us for weekly karaoke nights, live game watch parties, and special events throughout the year. Bring your friends, grab the mic, or cheer on your favorite team—we’re all about good vibes and unforgettable nights.
