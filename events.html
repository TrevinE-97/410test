Karaoke Night

Every Tuesday • 8PM - Midnight

Join us every Tuesday night for karaoke at Bench Warmers. Grab the mic, sing your favorite songs, and enjoy a fun night with friends, food, and cold drinks.

Saturday nights feature live bands at Bench Warmers. Come out for live music, a fun crowd, and the perfect weekend atmosphere to relax and enjoy the night.
